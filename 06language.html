  • Language

    06

    Visit

    The language of "Law of the Jungle" is willingly ironic and provocative, with a strong link to Instagram: from the colours and the gradients, and the square-cut for some images, to the format of Instagram posts and stories; all of these characteristics are taken from the social platform and then elaborated or sometimes exasperated. Another adapted language is that of the icons with the gradient, used as marks of the level of progress in the quiz, and also as stickers that self-censor some inappropriate photos. In this way, the users enter the website, feel part of the Instagram community and recognize the platform, despite the absurdity and extravagance with which "Law of the Jungle" presents itself. The website is realized with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Jquery, other libraries and plugins (Bootstrap, jInvertScroll).
